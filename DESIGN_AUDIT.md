## Key Design Principles Applied:
1. **Left-Anchoring**: Headers and hero sections use `justify-start` to stay grounded on the left grid.
2. **Unified Framing**: Every page uses the `.page-container` class (max-w 1800px) to match the `TopNav`.
3. **Architectural Density**: Bold, black typography (`font-black`) and mono labels create a mission-critical feel.
4. **Structural Clarity**: `arch-card` with consistent borders and backgrounds provides depth without clutter.
5. **Dynamic Depth**: Subtle background glows and blurred elements are positioned to enhance focus without interfering with content.
